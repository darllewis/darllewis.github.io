My research applies formal and empirical techniques to study federalism and intergovernmental relations. I explore federalism and intergovernmental relations, with a particular focus on the United States of America. The United States has successfully maintained a federated system of dual sovereignty between the states and the national government for nearly 250 years. My research develops models to understand the mechanisms driving this success with an eye towards the current state of other nations, including several Commonwealth states and the European Union.
